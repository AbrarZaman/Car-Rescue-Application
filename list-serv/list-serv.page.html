Overheating:

Solution:

Show Steps

Step 1

Stop the car and turn off the engine immediately.

Step 2

Allow the engine to cool down before opening
the hood.

Step 3

Check the coolant level and top it up if
necessary.

Step 4

If the problem persists, consult a mechanic for
further inspection.

Flat tyre:

Solution:

Show Steps

Step 1

Find a safe location and park the car.

Step 2

Locate the spare tyre, jack, and lug wrench.

Step 3

Jack up the car and remove the flat tyre.

Step 4

Replace it with the spare tyre and tighten
the lug nuts.

Cruise control failure:

Solution:

Show Steps

Step 1

Check if the cruise control switch is turned on.

Step 2

Ensure that the speed control sensors are
clean and functioning.

Step 3

Check the fuse related to the cruise control
system.

Step 4

If the problem persists, have the cruise
control system checked by a professional.

Key stuck in the ignition:

Solution:

Show Steps

Step 1

Make sure the car is in "Park" or the correct
gear.

Step 2

Try turning the steering wheel left and right
while gently turning the key.

Step 3

Apply a small amount of lubricant to the key
and ignition switch.

Step 4

If all else fails, contact a locksmith or your
car manufacturer for assistance.

Car stuck in scenarios such as mud, sand, etc.

Solution:

Show Steps

Step 1

Assess the situation and determine the best
course of action.

Step 2

If possible, try rocking the car back and forth
by shifting between drive and reverse.

Step 3

Use traction mats or boards to provide grip
under the wheels.

Step 4

If unable to free the car, call for professional
towing assistance.
